Three steps to your streak

No account. No setup maze. Just download, tell Fiz your name, and play.

1

Make it yours

Enter your name, pick your difficulty (Casual, Normal, or Difficult), and set a custom app icon. Fiz uses your name in every message — it's a small thing that makes the whole game feel different.

2

Spin to discover

Pull down on the wheel and let it fly. It lands on your category — Entertainment, Science, History, and nine others. A question appears inline, right on the same screen. No loading, no modals.

3

Build your streak

Every correct answer extends your streak. Every streak is saved to your personal leaderboard. Break your record and Fiz celebrates with you — by name. Come back tomorrow and try for more.

Privacy First

Your game stays on your device

No account required. No cloud sync. No data sold. Your streaks, answers, and preferences never leave your phone — because they're yours.

📱

Local Storage Only

Everything — streaks, leaderboard, preferences — is stored on your device using Apple's SwiftData. We literally cannot access it.

🚫

No Account Ever

No email. No sign-in. No password. Download and play immediately. Your identity in Fiz is just your first name.

📊

Optional Analytics

Anonymous, opt-out analytics help us improve the app. We never collect your answers, username, or anything personal.

🗑️

Delete Everything

Reset individual category progress or wipe everything at once in settings. Uninstalling the app removes all data permanently.
