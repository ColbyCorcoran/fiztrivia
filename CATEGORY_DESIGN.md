## Color Palette Strategy

Using a vibrant, distinct color palette:
- Warm colors: Gold, Orange, Red, Pink
- Cool colors: Blue, Teal, Green, Turquoise
- Neutral/Rich: Purple, Brown

Each category has a unique color to stand out on the wheel.
