Privacy Policy

We built Fiz around one idea: your game is yours. Here's exactly what that means.

Last Updated: April 26, 2026

The Short Version

  • Your game data stays on your device — we cannot access your streaks, answers, or preferences
  • No account or personal information is ever required
  • Analytics are optional and anonymous — no personal data or game content is collected
  • You control all data through app settings and can delete everything at any time
  • Expansion pack purchases are handled securely through Apple, not us

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Fiz collects, uses, and protects user information while maintaining our commitment to user privacy and data security.

Core Privacy Commitment

We cannot access your game data. All progress, streaks, answered questions, preferences, and leaderboard entries stay locally on your device, remaining completely inaccessible to the app developers.

Information Collection

Game Data (Local Storage Only)

The following data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework:

  • Answered Questions: IDs of questions you've answered to prevent duplicates
  • Leaderboard Entries: Your streak achievements with dates and game modes
  • Current Streak: Active streak count that persists across app sessions
  • Question History: Record of questions you've encountered

User Preferences (Local Storage Only)

  • Username: Optional personalized name for in-game messages
  • App Icon: Your selected app icon variant
  • Difficulty Mode: Chosen difficulty setting (Casual, Normal, Difficult)
  • Game Mode: Selected mode (Multi-Category, Single Category, Single Topic)
  • Category Selection: Which categories are enabled for gameplay
  • Phobia Filters: Topics you've chosen to exclude from questions
  • Haptic Feedback: Vibration preference settings
  • Swipe Navigation: Gesture control preferences
  • Popup Duration: Answer result display timing
  • Analytics Consent: Whether you've enabled or disabled analytics

Expansion Pack Data

  • Purchase History: Managed through your Apple ID and Apple's In-App Purchase system
  • Installed Packs: IDs of expansion packs you've chosen to install (stored locally)
  • Pack Progress: Answered questions from expansion packs (stored locally)

Analytics (Optional)

Enabled by Default: Analytics are enabled by default to help us improve the app, but you can disable them at any time in Settings. No personal information or game content is ever collected.

PostHog may collect:

  • App opens and backgrounding events
  • Feature interactions (button taps, settings changes)
  • Category completion milestones
  • Game mode usage
  • Expansion pack views and purchases
  • Settings changes
  • Performance metrics

PostHog never collects:

  • Question answers or results
  • Username or personal identifiers
  • Specific question content you've viewed
  • Leaderboard data or streak values
  • Purchase details beyond pack ID

Data Usage

Your locally stored data is used to:

  • Track which questions you've answered
  • Calculate and display streaks
  • Maintain leaderboard history
  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Filter questions based on difficulty and phobia preferences
  • Provide personalized in-game messages (when username is provided)

Optional analytics help us:

  • Understand which features are used
  • Identify performance issues
  • Improve app functionality
  • Prioritize new features

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your data.

The only third-party service with potential access to data is:

  • PostHog: GDPR-compliant analytics platform that receives anonymous usage statistics only when you enable analytics

Data Security

  • Local Storage: All game data benefits from iOS device encryption and remains on your device under your control.
  • No Cloud Transmission: Game progress, streaks, and preferences are never transmitted to remote servers.
  • Apple IAP Security: Expansion pack purchases are secured through Apple's In-App Purchase system.
  • Device Access: Data remains accessible only through your device's unlock mechanisms (Face ID, Touch ID, passcode).

Your Rights & Controls

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: View all data through the app (leaderboard, question history, settings)
  • Delete: Reset individual category or topic progress through settings, or use "Reset All Progress" to clear everything
  • Uninstall: Removing the app deletes all local data permanently
  • Export: Data remains on your device and is included in standard iOS backups
  • Analytics Control: Enable or disable analytics at any time in settings. Disabling analytics clears any previously collected data.
  • Phobia Filters: Add or remove content filters for specific topics at any time

Children's Privacy

Fiz does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app:

  • Does not require user accounts
  • Does not collect personal identification
  • Does not enable communication with other users
  • Stores all data locally on the device

Parents should monitor their children's use of in-app purchases and can use Screen Time restrictions to control purchases.

Data Retention

  • Local Data: Remains on your device until you delete it through the app or uninstall the app.
  • Analytics: When enabled, PostHog retains anonymous usage data according to their retention policies. No personal information or game content is included.
  • Purchase History: Managed by Apple through your Apple ID according to Apple's policies.

Third-Party Services

Apple Services

  • SwiftData: Local database framework that stores data on your device
  • StoreKit: In-App Purchase system for expansion packs (governed by Apple's privacy policy)
  • Family Sharing: Allows sharing of purchases with family members (governed by Apple's privacy policy)

PostHog (Optional Analytics)

  • Privacy-Focused: GDPR-compliant analytics platform
  • Anonymous Data: No personal identifiers or game content collected
  • User Control: Can be disabled at any time, which clears stored data

International Users

Since all data is stored locally on your device and not transmitted to remote servers:

  • Data remains in your physical possession
  • No international data transfers occur for game data
  • Optional analytics (if enabled) may process data in the United States, but contain no personal information or game content

Legal Rights by Region

California Residents (CCPA)

You have the right to:

  • Know: What personal information is collected (locally stored only, not transmitted)
  • Delete: Remove all local data by uninstalling or using in-app reset options
  • Opt-Out: We do not sell personal information

European Users (GDPR)

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all your data through the app
  • Rectification: Modify settings and preferences
  • Erasure: Delete data through app reset or uninstall
  • Portability: Data is included in iOS backups
  • Object: Disable analytics at any time

Since we don't collect or store your game data on remote servers, you maintain full control through the app and device.

Data Breach Response

In the unlikely event of a security incident, we will investigate promptly, notify affected users if personal data is compromised, and implement preventive measures.

Note: Since game data is stored locally on your device and not on our servers, the risk of a data breach affecting your game progress is minimal. Your data security primarily depends on your device security (passcode, Face ID, Touch ID).

Backup and Recovery

We recommend regular iOS device backups (iTunes or iCloud) and protecting your device with a passcode. We cannot recover lost game data if your device is lost, damaged, or if you uninstall without a backup.

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be indicated by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. We'll provide in-app notices for material changes. Continued use after changes indicates acceptance.
